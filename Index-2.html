What is Philosophy?

Philosophy is the study of the most basic questions about life, reality, knowledge, and values. It asks questions like “What is real?”, “What can we know?”, and “How should we live?” and seeks to answer them through careful thinking and reasoning rather than experiments. Essentially, philosophy is about examining ideas, questioning assumptions, and trying to understand the world and our place in it.
